Features

Everything to build real relationships at scale.

Not another CRM. A system designed for the people who actually talk to customers.

Digital Business Cards

Shareable, trackable cards with your photo, contact info, social links, and review profile. Share via text, email, or QR code. Know when they're viewed.

Personal Review Profiles

Collect reviews that live on your profile — not just the company's Google page. Your credibility is yours. It travels with you, job to job.

Congrats Cards

Every sale triggers a beautiful, branded card sent to your customer. It links to your review page, social profiles, and digital card — all in one tap.

Automated Campaigns

Birthday messages. Anniversary cards. Sold-date follow-ups. Holiday greetings. All automated, all personal. Pause or resume per contact with one tap.

Social Link Pages

One link for everything — your Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Google reviews, digital card, and showcase. Like a Linktree built for salespeople.

Leaderboards

See who's sending the most cards, collecting the most reviews, and driving the most engagement. Store-level, org-level, and global rankings.

Portable Reputation

Reviews that belong to people, not companies.

When a customer leaves a review, it lives on the salesperson's personal profile — not just on a company Google page. When that person moves to a new role, their reputation goes with them.

Reviews display on your digital business card
Personal rating score visible to customers
Stays with you if you switch companies
