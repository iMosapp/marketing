Cold Text Series

3-message drip
TEXT 1 Day 1 — The Opener

The "No Pitch" Intro

Hey [First Name] — quick question. How does your team stay in touch with customers after they buy? Not selling anything. Just curious because we built something that automates the entire follow-up process after a sale and dealers keep telling us it's the thing they didn't know they needed. — [Your Name]
Why it works: No pitch. Just curiosity. Dealers respond because the question is genuinely relevant to their daily problem.
TEXT 2 Day 3 — The Follow-Up

The "One Stat" Nudge

Hey [First Name] — following up. Quick stat: the average dealership loses 68% of customers to competitors within 2 years because nobody follows up after the sale. We fix that. Automatically. Takes 30 seconds to see how. Want me to send a quick video?
Why it works: One painful stat + a soft ask. "Send a video" feels low-commitment compared to "hop on a call."
TEXT 3 Day 7 — The Closer

The "Breakup" Text

Last one from me, [First Name]. If the timing's not right, no worries at all. But if you ever want to see how one photo with a customer can turn into 5 years of automated follow-up — I'm here. Either way, hope you're crushing it over there. [Link to your demo page]
Why it works: Breakup texts have the highest response rates. It's respectful, includes a link for later, and leaves on a high note.

The outreach playbook

How to run this campaign for maximum responses.

1

Build your list

Target GMs, Sales Managers, and BDC Directors at dealerships within 100 miles. Use LinkedIn, DealerRater, or Google Maps to find names.

2

Personalize each message

Mention their dealership name, a recent Google review, or something from their website. 30 seconds of research = 3x response rate.

3

Send Text 1 or Email 1 on a Tuesday

Tuesday-Thursday mornings (9-11am) have the highest open rates for B2B. Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons.

4

Follow the sequence timing

Day 1 → Day 3 → Day 7 for texts. Day 1 → Day 4 → Day 8 for emails. Don't skip the breakup — it converts the most.

5

Track who clicks your demo link

Use an i'M On Social tracking link for your demo page. You'll see exactly who's interested even if they don't reply.
