Install i'M On Social

Your relationship management portal. Add it to your home screen, it works just like an app.

You're already using the app! Open it from your home screen.

iPhone, 3 Easy Steps
1

Open in Safari

Make sure you're viewing this page in Safari (not Chrome or another browser).

2

Tap the Share button

It's the square with an arrow at the bottom of your screen. Scroll down in the menu.

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen" +

Hit "Add" in the top right. That's it, the app icon will appear on your home screen.

Android, 2 Easy Steps
1

Tap "Install" on the banner

Chrome will show an install banner at the bottom of your screen. Tap "Install" or "Add".

2

Confirm the install

Tap "Install" again on the confirmation popup. The app will appear on your home screen.

Desktop. Quick Install
1

Click the install icon in your address bar

Look for the small install icon (monitor with arrow) on the right side of Chrome's URL bar.

2

Click "Install"

The app will open in its own window and be available from your dock or start menu.
