The Process

How it works

Nothing changes about the photo. Everything changes about what happens after.

1

Salesperson takes the delivery photo

Same moment, same workflow. They open the app, snap the photo with the customer and their new vehicle. Takes 30 seconds.

2

Customer gets it instantly via text

A branded congrats card with their photo goes to the customer's phone right there at the dealership. They share it, save it, love it.

3

Review request goes out automatically

Minutes after the customer receives and saves their photo, a review request goes out with your dealership's branded logo. Timing is fully customizable per store, but we recommend within the first hour while the excitement is still fresh. More five-star reviews on autopilot.

4

Long-term follow-up kicks in

Birthdays, purchase anniversaries, service reminders. The platform handles it all, for years, automatically. Your salesperson builds a book of business without extra work.

5

Calendar Systems gets the data in real time

Customer name, phone number, stock number, deal number, the photo. All pushed into Calendar Systems the moment it happens. No CSVs. No waiting. No chasing.
