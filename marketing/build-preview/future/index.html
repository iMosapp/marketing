A NEW CATEGORY

The Future of Sales
Isn't CRM

It's Relationship Presence

For decades, sales teams have been told to use CRM systems.

But CRM was built for managers and reports, not for the people actually selling.

Salespeople don't lose customers because they lack a CRM.

They lose customers because they forget to follow up.

They get busy.

They move on to the next lead.

And the relationship disappears.

i'M On Social fixes that problem.

It turns every salesperson into a consistent, professional relationship builder — without requiring them to think about it.

The Problem With Sales Tools Today

Most tools focus on managing data.

  • storing contacts
  • logging notes
  • tracking opportunities
  • generating reports

But none of those things actually build relationships.

Relationships are built through consistent human touchpoints:

Texts Calls Emails Check-ins Follow-ups Reviews Social Sharing

Salespeople know this.

They just don't have a system that makes it easy.

A New Category: Relationship Operating System

i'M On Social isn't a CRM.
It's a Relationship Operating System.

Instead of asking salespeople to remember everything…

The system remembers for them.

Instead of asking them what to do next…

The system tells them.

Every day, the platform automatically generates Today's Touchpoints — a simple action list that tells the salesperson exactly who they should reach out to and how.

Text this customer.
Call that one.
Send a review request.
Share your card.

The salesperson just taps the task and the message is ready.

The relationship stays alive.

One Conversation Hub

All customer communication lives in one place.

Salespeople can send:

Text messages
Emails
Photos
Review links
Digital business cards

Everything is tracked automatically as a touchpoint, building a timeline of the entire relationship.

Managers finally gain visibility into who is actively communicating with customers — and who isn't.

No micromanaging required.

AI That Helps, Not Replaces

Inside every conversation is an AI assistant called Jessi.

Jessi can:

Suggest responses
Generate follow-up messages
Analyze conversation tone
Help craft the perfect reply

But the salesperson is always in control.

AI assists the relationship — it doesn't replace it.

Every Salesperson Gets a Personal Digital Presence

Each user has a digital business card page.

This page includes:

Their contact information
Dealership information
Customer reviews
Social links
A save-to-phone contact button

Salespeople can instantly share this page with customers by text or email.

It becomes their professional relationship hub.

The Hidden Power: Organic Search Growth

Something powerful happens when salespeople consistently use the platform.

Every interaction creates:

Review activity
Link sharing
Branded mentions
Indexed profile pages
Customer engagement signals

Over time this builds search authority for both the salesperson and the dealership.

Every salesperson becomes a search engine asset.

Instead of relying on a single dealership website, the dealership now has dozens of searchable profiles generating visibility.

And as AI-powered search grows, these relationship signals become even more important.

The result is powerful organic SEO and AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) generated naturally through daily sales activity.

No marketing campaigns required.

Just relationships.

Built-In Motivation Through Gamification

To keep teams engaged, the platform includes a leaderboard system.

Salespeople earn points for activities like:

Sending messages
Sharing digital cards
Collecting reviews
Completing touchpoint tasks

Levels, streaks, and rankings create healthy competition and encourage consistent engagement.

Managers can see real activity across their team — instantly.
