It's a relationship management platform built for sales teams and businesses that grow through personal connections. Think of it as a CRM that actually helps you stay in touch — with digital business cards, AI-powered messaging, automated campaigns, and team leaderboards.
How is this different from a regular CRM?+
Most CRMs are built for data entry. i'M On Social is built for action. Every feature — from digital cards to AI follow-ups — is designed to help you DO something, not just log information. Plus, it works as a mobile app your team will actually use.
How fast can we get started?+
Most teams are up and running in under an hour. We'll help you set up your brand, import contacts, and configure your first campaign during onboarding.
Does my team need to be tech-savvy?+
Not at all. The app is designed to feel like using Instagram or iMessage. If your team can send a text, they can use i'M On Social. We also provide training and support.
